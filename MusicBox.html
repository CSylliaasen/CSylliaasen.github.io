Music Box

This music box I made for my wife to commemorate our 5 year wedding anniversary. I wanted to do something really special and personalized that she would cherish forever. The face of the music box is a laser-cut piece of bamboo depicting the alignment of the planets (and pluto, even if it's not technically a planet) at the exact moment we said "I do". The interior is a felt insert designed to hold anniversary rings with the idea that every year I would get her another ring that she can place in the box. Underneath the insert is an MP3 board which has been configured to play a music box version of our song, "Such Great Heights".
