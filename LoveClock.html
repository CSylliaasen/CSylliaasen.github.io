"The Things I Love About You" Clock

The "Love Clock" is an anniversary I gift I made to show my wife all of the different things I love about her. Every hour, the clock selects one adjective and one noun at random and lights them up with random colors. In addition to the adjectives and nouns, there are special pre-programmed shapes that will appear like a red heart or a yellow waffle pattern. It's powered by an arduino nano and a series of addressable LED's. The address of each LED for each word is stored in an array which is assigned an integer. The arduino generates an integer at random and then displays the word which correlates with that integer.
