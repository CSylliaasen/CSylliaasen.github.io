Color Clock

"So many colors!"

The "Color Palette Clock", or "Color Clock" for short, is a graduation gift I made for my youngest brother's college graduation. The face is divided into 12 evenly spaced segments, like a traditional clock, except that each segment corresppnds to one color on a color wheel. Each segment lights up when either the short hand or long hand of a normal clock would fall in that area. In addition to the panels, there is an LCD display in the center of the face which shows the actual time. It is powered by an arduino nano but the time keeping is done by an additional time keeping unit with a quartz crystal. This is necessary because the internal timekeeping of the arduino is based on the number of completed cycles and it would not be very accurate at keeping time in the long run.
